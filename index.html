Objective:

The goal of the game is to break as many blocks as you can before missing the ball.

  • Your remaining lives are displayed top left
  • Your score is displayed top right
  • Your highest score is stored in local storage.

Rules:

  • You have 3 lives.
  • Use the space bar to pause the game.
  • If you miss the ball, your will reduce by 1.
  • Upon 3 misses, it's game over!

How To Play:

  • Use the Spacebar key to start, pause or resume the game.
  • Use your right and left arrow keys to move the paddle.
